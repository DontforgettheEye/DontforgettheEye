Greig Roulston

Python Developer, Artist, Designer, Maker of Things

Avid problem solver with over five years experience writing Python and Javascript for various functions including automation tasks, web apps, conversion pipelines and data analysis.

A keen learner who thrives on learning new technologies and skills, also very much enjoys teaching and presenting these learnings to others including conference presentations at Kiwi Pycon and the National Digital Forum . The only thing better than getting something to work is empowering someone else to do the same.

Along with technical skills I also thrive in creative areas with a strong understanding of visual communication design and love a good graph. I have worked as a concept artist, illustrator and have done my fair share of graphic design and web design work over the years.

A big fan of tinkering with technology I have taught myself GDscript for the Godot game engine, built racing drones, guitar effect pedals and built a few Arcade gaming machines.
